About Me

Hi! I'm Rama. I'm a tech enthusiast based in Jakarta, Indonesia. My professional interests include (but not limited to) telecommunications, internet of things (IoT), and IT infrastructure.

I graduated from Bandung Institute of Technology (ITB) with a bachelor's degree in Telecommunication Engineering. I focused my studies on telecommunications hardware development, communication systems, and radar engineering. Besides academic acitivities, I was also engaged in research and competitive projects related to RF engineering, IoT, and robotics software engineering. In addition, I also acquired some knowledge of computer networks, cloud computing, and data analysis from my studies.

After my studies, I participated in a radar research & development project backed by Indonesian Ministry of Defence, leading a team that developed radar antennas. Then I pursued an IT career and I'm still learning new IT stuffs until now.

Currently I'm interested in careers in telecommunications (Radio Engineer, Network Engineer), IoT (IoT Engineer, Embedded Systems Engineer), and IT infrastructure (Cloud Computing, DevOps, SRE). Reach me out if you're interested to collaborate with me!
