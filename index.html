About Me

Hi! I'm Rama. I'm an engineer and tech enthusiast. Currently I'm working in Bandung, Indonesia as an IoT engineer at a leading industrial IoT solutions provider in Southeast Asia, focusing on vehicle telematics. My professional interests include (but not limited to) telecommunications, internet of things (IoT), and IT infrastructure.

I graduated from Bandung Institute of Technology (ITB) with a bachelor's degree in Telecommunication Engineering. During my studies, I focused myself on telecommunications hardware development, communication systems, and radar engineering. Besides academic acitivities, I was also engaged in research and competitive projects related to RF engineering, IoT, and robotics software engineering. In addition, I also acquired knowledge and hands-on experience of computer networks, cloud computing, and data analysis throughout my studies and career.

After my studies, I participated in a radar research & development project backed by Indonesian Ministry of Defence, leading a team that developed radar antennas. Then I pursued an IT career, worked on network traffic analysis for some time, before landing a job in industrial IoT field.

I'm interested in careers in telecommunications (Radio Engineer, Network Engineer), IoT (IoT Engineer, Embedded Systems Engineer), and IT infrastructure (Cloud Computing, DevOps, SRE). Reach me out if you're interested to collaborate with me!
